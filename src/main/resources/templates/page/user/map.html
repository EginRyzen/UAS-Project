Our Story

We are a provider of boarding rooms (kos) committed to offering comfortable and affordable living spaces for students and professionals.

This website was originally created in 2025 as part of a college assignment. Over time, the project evolved into a more serious and professional platform. With continuous support and feedback from users, we have expanded our reach and now manage multiple boarding properties across various regions, striving to deliver practical and reliable housing solutions.
